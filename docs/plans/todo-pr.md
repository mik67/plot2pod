# plot2pod — Promotion & Growth Ideas

## Content-first (easiest wins)
- Post short audiogram clips (30–60s) from each podcast to Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts — auto-generate with a tool like Headliner or Descript
- Share the debate topic + key arguments as a Twitter/X thread
- Post "AI debated X today — here's what it said" style hooks

## Reddit (high potential)
- Find subreddits matching each podcast topic (e.g. /r/science, /r/politics, /r/technology) and share the episode as a discussion starter — not as spam, as genuine conversation
- /r/podcasts for visibility

## Facebook
- Topic-specific groups — same approach as Reddit, genuine participation
- A plot2pod Facebook page for regular episode posts

## SEO / passive discovery
- Submit RSS feed to Spotify, Apple Podcasts, Google Podcasts — free and drives long-tail discovery
- Guest posts on topic-adjacent blogs linking back to episodes

## Growth hack angle
- Let users share their own submitted topic once it's live — "I submitted this topic, listen to the debate" — personal investment drives sharing
- Add an email notification option so submitters get excited to share when their episode drops

## The core hook to use everywhere
> "AI debates any topic — submitted by real people, free to listen"

That angle is genuinely novel and shareable.
